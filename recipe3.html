Chilaquiles Recipe

History

Chilaquiles originate with the Aztecs.The name comes from the Nahuatl word "chilaquiles," which means "chilis and greens". The dish evolved from a resourceful way to use stale tortillas, a practice of pre-Columbian civilizations that transformed day-old tortillas with native ingredients like tomatoes, tomatillos, and chili peppers.

Some ingredients needed

All Ingredients
  • Chicken Breast
  • 2 tomates
  • 3 green tomates
  • 3 jalapenos
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Directions
  • In a pot, put the chicken breast to boil. Once done let it cool down and take it apart
  • In a different pot put 3 green tomatoes and 2 red ones to boil. Once cooked put them in the blender to make the salsa.
  • In a bigger pot, put a little bit of oil and let it heat up. Then grab your salsa, the chicken, and a bag of totops from the Ranchero brand.
  • Finally, put everything in the epot and let it boil for 5 minutes. Then, enjoy!
